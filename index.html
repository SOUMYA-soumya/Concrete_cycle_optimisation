Core Architecture & Business Impact

Heterogeneous ML Ensemble

Utilizes a tri-model Voting Regressor (HistGradientBoosting, ExtraTrees, GradientBoosting) trained on dynamically synthesized features like Water-to-Binder ratios to achieve >92% predictive accuracy.

Thermodynamic Simulation

Integrates the established Nurse-Saul Maturity Equation to mathematically simulate non-linear hydration rates based on local site weather, tracking equivalent age over real time.

Capital Allocation Engine

Calculates the precise Pareto frontier between cycle speed and overhead cost, determining exactly when the heavy energy costs of steam curing become mathematically profitable.
